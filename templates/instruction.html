What you need to know.
The Digital Strategy Planning Tool, The Digital AMPS Approach and the StratahedronTM Strategy Tool.
The Digital AMPs Approach helps you identify and articulate the key elements of your business and how you deliver your remarkable value.
There are four elements that are part of the Digital Expedition of Transformation. Aligning your elements creates a strong foundation, structure and architecture for defining what is important and how you deliver your remarkable value.
The Four Elements of the AMPS StratahedronTM are:
[A] Audience -who you serve and how you communicate your value
[M] Method -the internal structures that you use to deliver your value
[P] Process -the external steps that you guide your audience through
[S] Story - the journey that you are taking your audience on
Within each of the 4 elements of the AMPS StratahedronTM are three sub-elements that define the parameters of each of your main elements. These sub-elements further identify the course of actions that can be taken to further align and identify your AMPS structure
When these four elements are defined and physically connected together, they are able to form a unique, 3-Dimensional 4-sided pyramid shape, that is unique to you and only your business. This shape is called a Tetrahedron, and it contains your Strategic DNA - the core of what you do and how you deliver your value. When it is combined with your strategic elements, it becomes a StratahedronTM- your 3-Dimensional structure of your strategy. While the 4 sided shape is similar to all businesses and a foundation for all, only you have the defined structure within the StratahedronTM as you are able to create it specifically for you and your business.
Within the larger structure of the 4 triangles, are the internal structures that this model creates, expands and demonstrates, once in the 3-D shape, they become the core vertices of:
1. Clarity
2. Certainity
3. Competency
Finally, the Content #Hashtags are content topics that are an integral part of your strategy model. These are concepts that are connecting the pieces of the model together through the main vertices - topics that you can carry forward and that can pull your audience and community together.
This is your StratahedronTM creator studio to show you how to design your own StratahedronTM. Once you have created your strategy model design, you can print it out or re-design another one.
The StratahedronTM is a design strategy tool to help you see and visualize your core digital business strategy. It allows you to develop and then clearly to see what is important and how it fits together, where the intersections are, and how to get clarity with your business in a multitude of directions.
It takes the four major elements of Audience, Method, Process & Story, and combines them into a strategic architecture that you can then use to identify, plan and execute projects as part of your Digital Expedition of Transformation & journey of digital transformation.
After entering the details of your AMPS StratahedronTM - you can then print it out and create your own, individual StratahedronTM that can then be assembled into the Tetrahedron shape. You can also email the model to yourself, to print and assemble more StratahedronsTM
